Technical Support

If you are looking for hardware that supports a specific Khronos standard such as OpenGL, please refer to the Frequently Asked Questions page. On this page, choose the Khronos standard in which you are interested, then the page will list the products that have been verified to be conformant with that standard. Questions about the hardware should be addressed to the hardware manufacturer.

For questions about software, Khronos is a consortium of companies that defines standards, but we do not make any products around them. Instead we maintain the standards which hardware companies support with drivers, and which software companies implement in their own products.
